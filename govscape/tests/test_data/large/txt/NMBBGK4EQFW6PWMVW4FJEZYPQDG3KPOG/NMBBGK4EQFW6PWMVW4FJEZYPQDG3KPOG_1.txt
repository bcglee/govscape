Mexi-Mashed Potatoes
2 cups water 1. In medium saucepan, bring water and margarine
3 tablespoons margarine or butter to a rolling boil.
3/4 cup milk 2. Remove from heat and stir in milk and potato
2 cups potato flakes flakes with fork until potatoes are of desired
4 oz. (1 cup) shredded Cheddar cheese consistency.
1 teaspoon chili powder 3. Stir in cheese, chili powder, and oregano.
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
Makes six ½-cup servings
Variation:
• For a healthier choice, choose a low-fat or reduced
calorie cheese.
• If desired, garnish with diced tomato, chopped
green onions, and cheese.
• For an extra flavor burst, substitute Taco-flavored or
Mexican blend cheese for Cheddar cheese.
Nutrition Information for each serving of Mexi-Mashed Potatoes:

Saturated Fat 5.0 g Dietary Fiber 1 g Vitamin C 9 mg
Chantilly Potatoes
1. Preheat oven 450°F
6 servings prepared instant mashed potatoes
2. Spread mashed potatoes in a 2 quart casserole.
1 cup diced ham
Cover with ham.
1/2 cup whipping cream
3. Whip cream until stiff; fold in cheese. Spread
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
mixture over ham. Bake for 10-15 minutes or
until golden brown.
Makes 8 servings
Nutrition Information for each serving of Chantilly Potatoes:
Calories 210 Cholesterol 42 mg Sugar 4 g Calcium 135 mg
Calories from Fat 125 Sodium 590 mg Protein 9 g Iron 0 mg
Total Fat 14 g Total Carbohydrate 14 g Vitamin A 110 RAE
Saturated Fat 7 g Dietary Fiber 2 g Vitamin C 5 mg
