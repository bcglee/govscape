4.7 MCCAMMON BRANCH
Site 7 is located approximately 1 mile west of Atkinstown in the southern half of Jackson
County. The dam would be situated on McCammon Branch approximately half a mile upstream
from the confluence with the Laurel Fork River. The pool elevation of the reservoir would be
approximately 1,160 feet above MSL.
