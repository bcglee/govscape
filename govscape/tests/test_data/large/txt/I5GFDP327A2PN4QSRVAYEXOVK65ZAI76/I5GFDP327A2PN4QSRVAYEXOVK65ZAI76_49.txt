4.9 WAR FORK AND ALCORN BRANCH
Site 9 is located approximately one mile east of Wind Cave in eastern Jackson County. The dam
would be situated on War Fork approximately 2 miles south of the confluence with South Fork.
The pool elevation of the reservoir would be approximately 795 feet above MSL.
