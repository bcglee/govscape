4.5 SOUTH FORK OF STATION CAMP CREEK AND CAVANAUGH CREEK #2
Site 5 is located in the northeastern corner of Jackson County approximately 2.5 miles southeast
of the town of Drip Rock. The dam would be situated on the South Fork just below the
confluence with Cavanaugh Creek. The pool elevation of the reservoir would be approximately
820 feet above MSL.
