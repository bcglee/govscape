4.13 STURGEON CREEK
Site 13 is located near the Jackson/Owsley County boundary line in eastern Jackson County and
is located approximately 1.5 miles northeast of the town of Mummie. The dam would be
situated on Sturgeon Creek just below the confluence with Blackwater Creek. The pool
elevation of the reservoir would be approximately 980 feet above MSL.
