4.8 MILL CREEK
Site 8 is located approximately 3 miles south of the town of McKee in the southern half of
Jackson County. The dam would be situated on Mill Creek near the Highway 290 bridge. The
pool elevation of the reservoir would be approximately 1,135 feet above MSL.
