4.4 SOUTH FORK OF STATION CAMP CREEK AND ROCK LICK CREEK
Site 4 is located in the northeastern corner of Jackson County approximately 1 mile southeast of
the town of Drip Rock. The dam would be situated on the South Fork near the Highway 89
bridge. The pool elevation of the reservoir would be approximately 900 feet above MSL.
