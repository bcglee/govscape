Tumbling For Seed Cleaning And Conditioning
By: David Dreesen2
Study Number: NMPMC-T-0501-OT
ABSTRACT
Small rock tumblers can be used to clean and condition seeds both in an aqueous and a dry mode.
During the process, grit and gravel remove fruit pulp and abrade seed coats. Wet tumbling of seed
aids imbibition, leaches water-soluble germination inhibitors, and may partially substitute for
cold stratification for some shrub seed lots.
Key Words:
Oleaceae, Forestiera pubescens var. pubescens, New Mexico olive, Plantanaceae, Platanus

torreyii, wolfberry, Cornaceae, Cornus sericea ssp. sericea, redosier dogwood
