SULFONAMIDES
FSIS analyzed 3,008 samples for sulfonamides. Seventeen (17) violations were detected
in seventeen (17) animals from several production classes. The chemical residue
violations consisted of three (3) sulfadimethoxine, and fourteen (14) sulfamethazine.
