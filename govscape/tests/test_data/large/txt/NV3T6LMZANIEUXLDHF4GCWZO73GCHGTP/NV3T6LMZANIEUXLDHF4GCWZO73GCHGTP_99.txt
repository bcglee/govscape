Bt3—37 to 45 inches; yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) silty clay loam; moderate medium
subangular blocky structure; friable; many distinct brown (10YR 4/3) clay films on
faces of peds; few medium prominent yellowish brown (10YR 5/8) masses of iron
accumulation in the matrix; slightly acid; clear wavy boundary.
2Bt4—45 to 55 inches; yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) clay loam; moderate medium
subangular blocky structure; friable; many distinct brown (10YR 4/3) clay films on
faces of peds; common medium prominent yellowish brown (10YR 5/8) masses of
iron accumulation in the matrix; neutral; clear wavy boundary.
2Bt5—55 to 60 inches; yellowish brown (10YR 5/4), stratified clay loam and sandy
loam; moderate medium subangular blocky structure; friable; many distinct brown
(10YR 4/3) clay films on faces of peds; common medium prominent yellowish
brown (10YR 5/8) masses of iron accumulation in the matrix; neutral.
