very strongly acid; clear smooth boundary.
2Bt4—27 to 36 inches; yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) clay loam; moderate medium and

yellowish brown (10YR 6/4) silt coats on faces of peds; few distinct brown (7.5YR

2Cd—56 to 72 inches; yellowish brown (10YR 5/4) loam; massive; firm; few fine
